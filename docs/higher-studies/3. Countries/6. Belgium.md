Belgian universities consistently rank well in global rankings and are respected worldwide for research, innovation, and academic excellence. The country is also safe, student-friendly, and home to international institutions such as the European Union and NATO, making it a truly global learning environment.

## Why Study in Belgium from Bangladesh

Studying in Belgium offers many advantages for Bangladeshi students:

- High-Quality Education System
  - World-class universities and research institutions
  - Strong focus on practical learning and innovation
- Global Recognition of Belgian Degrees
  - Degrees accepted worldwide
  - Strong academic reputation across Europe and beyond
- English-Taught Programs
  - Many Bachelor’s and Master’s programs taught fully in English
  - Ideal for international students
- Multicultural and International Environment
  - Students from over 150 countries
  - Exposure to different cultures and languages
- Affordable Tuition Compared to Other EU Countries
  - Lower tuition fees than the UK, USA, and Australia
  - Good value for money
- Strong Job Market and Research Opportunities
  - Opportunities in engineering, IT, business, healthcare, and research
  - Close connections with European industries

## Step-by-Step Process to Study in Belgium from Bangladesh

### Step 1: Research Courses & Universities

Start by selecting the right course and university.

**Public vs Private Universities**

- Public universities: Lower tuition fees, highly ranked
- Private universities: Higher fees, limited programs

**Popular Fields for Bangladeshi Students**

- Engineering
- Business & Management
- Information Technology
- Health Sciences
- Architecture
- Social Sciences

**Language of Instruction**

- English (most international programs)
- French (Wallonia & Brussels)
- Dutch (Flanders)

### Step 2: Check Eligibility Requirements

**Academic Qualifications**

- Bachelor’s: SSC + HSC
- Master’s: Recognized Bachelor’s degree

**English Language Requirements**

- IELTS: Usually 6.0 – 6.5 overall
- TOEFL: Accepted by many universities

**French/Dutch Programs**

- Language proficiency certificates may be required

**Work Experience**

- Required for some professional Master’s programs (e.g., MBA)

### Step 3: Prepare Required Documents

Common documents include:

- Academic transcripts & certificates
- Valid passport
- Updated CV
- Statement of Purpose (SOP)
- Letters of Recommendation
- Language test results

### Step 4: Apply to Belgian Universities

- Applications are submitted online
- Deadlines vary by university (usually January–April)
- Application fees may apply

### Step 5: Receive Offer Letter

- Conditional offer: Requirements still pending
- Unconditional offer: All conditions met
- Accept the offer within the deadline

### Step 6: Pay Tuition Fee (Initial Deposit)

- Partial payment or first-year tuition required
- Payment via bank transfer
- Amount depends on the university

### Step 7: Apply for Belgium Student Visa from Bangladesh

**Visa Type**

- Type D (Long-Stay Student Visa)

**Required Documents**

- Admission letter
- Proof of tuition payment
- Valid passport
- Medical certificate
- Police clearance
- Proof of accommodation

**Proof of Funds**

- Bank statement
- Blocked account
- Financial sponsorship
- Visa Processing Time
- Usually 4–8 weeks

### Step 8: Accommodation & Living Arrangements

**Accommodation Options**

- University dormitories
- Private student housing
- Shared apartments

**Average Rent**

- €300 – €600 per month

### Step 9: Pre-Departure Preparation

- Book flights early
- Arrange health insurance
- Exchange currency
- Prepare a packing checklist

### Step 10: Arrival in Belgium & Post-Arrival Steps

- Register at local municipality
- Apply for residence permit
- Complete university registration
- Open a bank account

## Pros and Cons of Studying in Belgium

**Pros**

- High-quality education
- Affordable tuition compared to many EU countries
- Multilingual exposure
- Strong research and innovation environment

**Cons**

- Language barriers in daily life
- Competitive admissions
- Limited part-time jobs in some regions

## Scholarships for Bangladeshi Students in Belgium

- Belgian Government Scholarships
- Erasmus+ Program
- University-specific scholarships
- Regional scholarships (Flanders, Wallonia, Brussels)

**Funding Types:**

- Partial funding
- Full funding (limited availability)

- Full-time during holidays
